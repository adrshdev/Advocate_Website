Adv. Rohan Menon

Adv. Rohan Menonis a dedicated legal professional with over 8 years of experience representing clients across civil, criminal and property law matters.
A graduate of Government Law College, Ernakulam, he is known for his strong courtroom presence, ethical approach and client- first attitude.
With a deep understandings of the Indian legal system, Adv. Menon has successfully handled a wide range of cases, from complex property disputes to sensitive family matters. His commitment to justice and tireless work ethic has earned him the trust of individuals and businesses alike.
He is a registered member of the Bar Council of Kerala and regularly provides legal consultation to help clients make informed decisions before pursuing litigation.
When he is not in court, Rohan believes in empowering people through legal awareness and contribute to community-based legal education initiatives.
